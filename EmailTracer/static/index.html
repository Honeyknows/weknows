How It Works

Three simple steps to uncover the truth behind any email.

1

Extract Headers

Open the suspicious email in your client (Gmail, Outlook) and copy the raw, original message headers.

2

Run Analysis

Paste the headers into EmailTracer. Our engine parses the routing, performs DNS lookups, and validates auth records.

3

Review Evidence

Instantly receive a comprehensive risk score, IOC extraction, and a chronological routing timeline to take action.
